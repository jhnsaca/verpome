The Founder

Grégoire
Thibaut.
Builder.

Born in Lyon, shaped by the world. Studies took him through Europe, Africa and Brazil — each country a different visual language, a different way of seeing light, movement, and stories worth telling. The rest followed naturally, passport stamp by passport stamp.

A lifelong obsession with cinema, aesthetics and the art of visual storytelling became Verpome Studio. Years of creating — reels, films, photography, web — built an eye that no classroom can teach. Marathon finisher. Multi-sport athlete. Someone who approaches every project the way he approaches a race: with discipline, patience, and the intention to finish strong.
